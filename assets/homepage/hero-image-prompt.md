## Purpose
The single flagship image of the EZOrders Universe. First impression of the whole brand. It must make a restaurant owner feel "I want my restaurant to feel like that" in under one second, before reading any words. The restaurant is the hero; the owner is the winner; the software is invisible except one heartbeat of indigo light.

## Camera
Camera A — The Establishing. Eye-level +, height 1.75m, ~26mm equivalent, positioned camera-left-of-center looking across the oak-and-concrete service counter toward the open kitchen, capturing the full left-to-right journey. Verticals true, no distortion, deep-ish focus: counter tack-sharp, dining foreground melting gently.

## Lighting
L-GoldenHour. Low amber sun rakes through the street frontage, long warm shadows across honed concrete, brushed brass glowing, soft interior downlights plus the cove behind the counter.

## Palette grade
Warm neutrals, honey oak, pale concrete, amber golden-hour key. Indigo #3B33C8 reserved exclusively for the Purple Pulse and light lines. A whisper of tint #FEEFF3 in the banquette. No competing color noise.
